Copenhagen

Come to Denmark, and join us in beautiful, historic Copenhagen. One of the greenest cities in the world, Copenhagen is extremely bike friendly and has excellent public transport. It's well-connected, with an international airport and a bridge to Sweden just south of the city. Most importantly (for Joe, at least) it has truly excellent bakeries.
